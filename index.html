How we solve the Problem

Pillar No. 1

Near-Peer Mentorship

Start with the basics through guided sessions with mentors who've walked your path. We cover AI fundamentals in practical, relatable terms that make sense to beginners and

Pillar No. 2

Guest Sessions

Get real-world insights and training from professionals actively working in AI and tech. Learn about what's actually happening in the industry, not just theory from textbooks.

Pillar No. 3

Hands-On Learning

Put knowledge into practice with real use cases of Generative AI tools. Build your own simple custom AI agents and discover how AI can amplify your existing skills.

Apply everything you've learned by building a passion project that combines AI tools with your domain expertise. It's that's social work, literature, business, or any field that you're passionate about.
